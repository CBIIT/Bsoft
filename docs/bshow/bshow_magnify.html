The Magnify window

This window shows a magnification of a 2D image, or 3 orthogonal slices through a 3D map, centered on the current mouse location. It also features animation controls to pan through slices at a desired rate.


Information and control:

  • x and y mouse position, optionally showing the middle crosses
  • Size and magnification
  • Animation range (Â±slices), speed, a toggle button and a direction
  • Current slice indicator

Key mapping:

  • l - toggles the lock on the xy position used for magnification
  • m - decreases the magnification
