14 @Object: class Bsymop
15 @Description:
16  General symmetry operator.
17 @Features:
18  Both point groups and space groups are covered.
19  A symmetry operator describes the complete series of rotations around
20  an axis, with the number of operations given by the order field
21  and the rotation angle given by the angle field. The shift field
22  is used in gliding operations, such as needed for helical symmetry.
23 *************************************************************************/
