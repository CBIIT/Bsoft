int img_diffraction_origin ( Bimage *  p,
Bimage *  pmask 
)

Finds the origin of a powder diffraction pattern by cross-correlation.

Parameters
*pimage.
*pmaskmask to exclude a region such as a beam stop.
Returns
int 0. 
The diffraction pattern is copied, masked around the set origin to 
remove background distractions, and cross-correlated with a copy of 
itself rotated 180 degrees. To overcome the masking bias, the 
masking and cross-correlation are repeated until the result stabilizes.
The origins within the sub-image structures are used for masking.
The new origins are written back into the sub-image structures.
